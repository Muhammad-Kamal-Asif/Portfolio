My Story

Hi! I’m Muhammad Kamal, a passionate BBA student with a heart in community building and a mind in creativity and data. I’ve always believed in merging purpose with performance — that’s what led me to graphic design, bookkeeping, and front-end development.

From managing real events at Lincoln Corner to building UI prototypes and editing cultural videos, I’ve had the chance to wear multiple hats — and I enjoy it. Learning by doing has been my way to grow, with over 50 certifications supporting this journey.

Now, I’m looking forward to contributing to organizations where I can blend my creativity, analytical thinking, and business acumen to create meaningful impact.
